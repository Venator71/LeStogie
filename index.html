Maduro

Maduro means “ripened” or “matured.” Therefore, Maduro wrappers are those using aged leaves that undergo a natural fermentation process that results in a stunning dark brown leaf.

Connecticut

Connecticut wrapper leaves are sourced from the Connecticut River Valley. This region is a core part of America’s tobacco-growing industry. You’ll recognize these wrappers from their lighter shade.

Corojo

Originally grown in Cuba, this slightly darker leaf is now grown mostly in Honduras. This unique leaf is renowned for a particularly spicy and peppery quality.

Dense with nicotine, Habano has a spicy and heavy quality preferred by some smokers. It's so rich; it may be intimidating to people who are new to cigars.
